About ORUM

ORUM isn't just another School ERP. We are a revolutionary platform designed for modern education. We seamlessly integrate complete school management with unparalleled learning visibility, ensuring every stakeholder—parents, teachers, and students—is empowered with transparent, real-time data.ORUM is a next-generation School ERP + Learning Visibility Platform designed to bridge the gap between classroom performance and real student effort. It combines school management, self-study tracking, and data transparency into one connected ecosystem for parents, teachers, and students. With ORUM, education becomes measurable, transparent, and truly student-centered.

What Our ERP Delivers

Our robust ERP module streamlines all academic operations, converting routine data into actionable insights for parents and faculty.

📚 Live Attendance

Instant synchronization between classroom attendance and the parent dashboard, ensuring transparency.

🧾 Fee Management

Complete transparency in all transactions, due dates, and digital receipts, accessible 24/7.

📢 Instant Communication

School circulars, notices, and important updates reach both students and parents in real-time.

📊 Academic Analytics

Our ERP auto-generates insightful performance graphs, correlating exam results with self-study time.

🧠 Behavioral Insights

Attendance patterns and assignment submissions provide teachers with data to track student engagement.

Core Platform Features

  • Daily Study Proof: Simple upload to verify session completion.
