About Parakk-ERP

ORUM is an upcoming Platform that connects - Schools/Teachers/Parents and Students transparently. It isn't just another School ERP or another self study measurement tool. We are a revolutionary platform designed for modern school management and students self education. We seamlessly integrate complete school management with unparalleled learning visibility, ensuring every stakeholder—parents, teachers, and students—is empowered with transparent, real-time data. ORUM is a next-generation School ERP + Learning Visibility Platform designed to bridge the gap between classroom performance and real student effort. It combines school management, self-study tracking, and data transparency into one connected ecosystem for parents, teachers, and students. With ORUM, education becomes measurable, transparent, and truly student-centered.

What Our ERP Delivers

Our robust ERP module streamlines all academic operations, converting routine data into actionable insights for parents and faculty.

📚 Live Attendance

Instant synchronization between classroom attendance and the parent dashboard, ensuring transparency.

🧾 Fee Management

Complete transparency in all transactions, due dates, and digital receipts, accessible 24/7.
